About Sneep It!

Description

"Sneep it" - this is a completely new platform with a huge base of ready-made templates for letters, documents, forms, code etc. Thanks to our platform, you will never again have questions like "What should look like an official letter", "How to politely refuse my boss" and much more... Just take and copy the template you need, now it is not a problem anymore!

Functions

1. Authentication and registration of users,
2. Adding, creating and editing mock-ups,
3. Adding your favorite snippets to your favorites,
4. Search.
